PVC GROWER FEEDER


The term Grower used to describe all stock between the end of brooding and till they reach sexual maturity. In grower poultry farms, birds are kept from 9 weeks to 16-18 weeks. They are also called replacement pullets. Feeding space per bird is to be kept 10cm. Watering space per bird is to be kept 2-2.5cm.
